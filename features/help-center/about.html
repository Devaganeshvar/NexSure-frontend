Who We Are

IPMS is India's trusted digital insurance platform — connecting policyholders, insurance officers, and administrators in one secure, transparent system built around simplicity and trust.

Our Story

Founded with a single belief — that insurance should be accessible, understandable, and stress-free — IPMS was built to eliminate the paperwork, confusion, and delays that have long defined the insurance industry in India.

From a first-time policyholder applying for life cover to a seasoned officer processing hundreds of claims a month, IPMS serves every actor in the insurance lifecycle with equal care and precision.
