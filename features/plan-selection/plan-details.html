Standard Health Plan

Comprehensive health coverage for you and your family, designed to provide peace of mind with cashless treatments at 10,000+ network hospitals.

Cashless Treatment Zero Copay Free Annual Checkups

What's Covered?

This plan provides extensive coverage for medical emergencies and planned hospitalizations. Below is a detailed breakdown of the coverage limits and inclusions.

In-Patient Hospitalization

Covered up to the Sum Insured. Includes room rent, ICU, nursing, and doctor fees.

Ambulance Cover

Up to ₹3,000 per hospitalization for road ambulance services.

Pre & Post Hospitalization

60 days pre-hospitalization and 90 days post-hospitalization medical expenses covered.

Day Care Procedures

All day care procedures (surgeries requiring <24 hrs stay) are fully covered.

Key Benefits

  • No Claim Bonus

    Increase your sum insured by 20% for every claim-free year, up to a maximum of 100%.

  • Restoration Benefit

    100% automatic restoration of sum insured if exhausted during the policy year.

  • Free Annual Checkup

    Free preventive health checkup for all insured members once a year.

  • Tax Benefits

    Save tax under Section 80D of the Income Tax Act.

Exclusions

Things that are not covered under this plan to keep premiums affordable:

  • Pre-existing diseases (covered only after 36 months waiting period).
  • Initial 30-day waiting period for any illness (except accidental injuries).
  • Cosmetic or plastic surgery unless necessitated by an accident or cancer.
  • Dental treatment or surgery unless requiring hospitalization.
  • Self-inflicted injuries or conditions arising from substance abuse.

How to Claim

Our claims process is fully digital, fast, and transparent. Follow these simple steps.

1
Intimate Claim

Notify us within 24 hours of hospitalization (for emergencies) or 48 hours prior (for planned admissions) via the app or toll-free number.

2
Submit Documents

Upload digital copies of your medical bills, discharge summary, and KYC documents through our portal.

3
Review & Approval

Our dedicated claims team will review your submission. For cashless network hospitals, approval takes less than 2 hours.

Settlement

For reimbursement claims, the approved amount will be directly credited to your registered bank account within 7 working days.
